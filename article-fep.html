Forward Energy Premium (FEP)

A single, forward-looking signal that tells you how energy, policy and climate exposure show up in ROI.

Investors Operators Signals 5 min read

What FEP is

FEP = Forward Energy Premium. It estimates the return impact from shared drivers (energy prices, policy, climate) and isolates what’s asset-specific. Think of it as an “exposure meter” that points to where value is most at risk—or most improvable.

Why it matters

  • Comparable across assets. A single signal you can rank on a portfolio page.
  • Forward, not backward. Built from plausible futures, not just last year’s bills.
  • Actionable. Links straight to retrofit bundles that move FEP the most per £.

How we compute it (conceptual)

  1. Model a few plausible futures (prices, grid carbon, weather).
  2. Simulate each asset’s costs/comfort under each future (“as-is”).
  3. Estimate sensitivity to shared drivers → β (systematic) and what remains → idiosyncratic.
  4. Summarise into a forward premium: expected impact and band of uncertainty.

Outcome: FEP number + band, plus β split (systematic vs idiosyncratic) shown on the portfolio screen.

What you see in Hoshi

  • FEP sparkline per building and a portfolio roll-up.
  • β split: how much is market-wide vs asset-specific.
  • Actions ranked by expected FEP improvement × confidence.

Why it’s better than static intensity or EPC alone

  • Intensity is descriptive; FEP is predictive and scenario-aware.
  • EPC is compliance; FEP speaks finance (exposure, bands, expected gain).
  • It ties directly to NPV and comfort, not just kWh/ft².

Use-cases

Portfolio triage

Sort by FEP to see where exposure is highest. Click through to actions that deliver the biggest improvement under most futures.

Capex planning

Compare bundles (“controls + LEDs” vs “ASHP + fabric”) by their FEP delta and scenario NPV band.

IC story

“Here’s the exposure, here’s how the bundle changes it, here’s the NPV band and assumptions.”

Quick start

  1. Upload 12–36 months of bills/meters + floor area + hours.
  2. Pick a target path; Hoshi sets default futures you can adjust.
  3. Open the portfolio → sort by FEP → shortlist actions.
