Hoshi in 5 minutes

From utility bills to decision-grade signals (NPV, β, systematic vs idiosyncratic).

5 min read Investors Operators Signals

One-liner

Hoshi is the decision layer for real-estate decarbonisation. It turns meter data and targets into investment-grade decisions: scenario stress-tests, retrofit economics (NPV/IRR), comfort risk, and IC-ready packs.

What Hoshi actually does

  • Ingests & cleans meters, tariffs, hours, targets.
  • Builds futures to 2050 (prices, grid carbon, weather) and runs your assets through each.
  • Prices risk like finance (β / forward premium), not just labels.
  • Ranks actions by scenario-robust NPV, comfort effect, and “no-regrets” vs “option bets.”

Buildings are few, big, and unique—you can’t diversify idiosyncratic risk away like a stock portfolio. You need asset-specific signals.

Why Hoshi is different

  • Forward, not just backward. Scenario bands (prices, policy, climate), not single-point dashboards.
  • Physics + finance. EPC-style view paired with an APT-style risk premium investors understand.
  • Comfort & resilience built-in. Overheating risk (adaptive comfort) for NV/mixed-mode stock.
  • Decision outputs. IC-ready narratives with methods & assumptions.

Market context: reporting platforms excel at disclosures; Hoshi’s edge is decision-grade, scenario-aware investment analysis (NPV/β/comfort → value). Use them together.

Use-cases (quick stories)

1) Portfolio triage (week 1)

Upload meters/targets → see where you’re off-track vs pathway and which sites have high β. One click reveals no-regrets actions that win in most futures.

2) Heat strategy (month 1)

Compare “stay on gas” vs “ASHP + controls.” In most plausible UK futures, grid carbon falls fast; electrifying heat cuts both carbon and risk, often with better lifecycle economics—Hoshi shows the NPV band and comfort effect.

3) IC pack (anytime)

Export the memo: baseline → scenarios → recommended bundle → NPV/IRR, β, comfort/overheating, sensitivities and assumptions—ready for committee.

Signals you’ll get

  • Forward Energy Premium (β split): systematic vs idiosyncratic drivers of value.
  • Scenario-NPV bands for each action/bundle.
  • Overheating hours / comfort badge (NV & mixed-mode).
  • Target gap & resilience (works in ≥6/8 futures).

Why now

  • Policy is tightening; EPC alone is a weak price signal—pair it with a financial, scenario-based view.
  • Grid carbon collapses in most futures by 2030–35 → electrification becomes a robust play.
  • Comfort risk is rising as summers warm; mixed-mode with shading/controls often wins.

What we need to start

Asset list, floor areas, 12–36 months of meters, tariffs, hours, target path (e.g., CRREM), and a shortlist of candidate measures. Hoshi does the rest—from raw data to decisions.
