Compare Buildings

Side-by-side decisions: photos, servicing, age, energy, carbon, intensity, and spend—on one screen.

Feature Tenants Owners Investors 5 min read

What it is

Buildings Comparison lets you select up to three assets and see them side-by-side with a photo header and the essentials: location, sector, servicing strategy, year built, area, energy, tCO₂e, intensity and spend. It’s built for quick triage and fast, shared understanding.

Who benefits

  • Tenants. Compare comfort context (servicing strategy, age), energy and carbon before shortlisting a space.
  • Owners/Operators. Spot which asset is most improvable and why—then jump to actions.
  • Investors. A crisp view of asset specifics you can’t diversify away; perfect for IC memos and portfolio reviews.

How it works in Hoshi

  1. Open Compare from the left nav.
  2. Select up to three buildings from the card grid (each card can show a photo).
  3. Review the side-by-side table: location, sector, servicing, year, area, energy (kWh), tCO₂e, intensity (kWh/m²), spend, last updated.

Tip: add 1–3 image URLs, year built and servicing strategy when creating a building. That context makes comparisons click for non-technical stakeholders.

Why it matters

  • Asset-specific clarity. Buildings are few, large and idiosyncratic; photos + servicing + KPIs show why assets differ.
  • Decision-ready. Pairs visual context with numbers that feed actions and planning.
  • Faster consensus. Tenants, owners and investors look at the same facts—less debate, more decision.

Use-cases

Leasing shortlist

Tenant rep compares three options. The naturally-ventilated, newer building shows lower intensity and overheating risk; photos confirm façade shading. Shortlist made.

Capex sprint

Owner compares two laggards and a median site. The gas-heavy building has the weakest intensity; actions for electrifying heat + controls get priority.

IC pack slide

Investor pulls the side-by-side into a memo with one paragraph of rationale and a link to actions and lineage.

What’s next

  • Comfort overlays. Warm-hours / overheating badges in the table.
  • Scenario tags. Show which assets win across most futures.
  • Export. One-click PNG/PDF for IC packs.

Quick start

  1. Add or edit buildings to include 1–3 images, year built, and servicing.
  2. Open Compare → select up to 3 assets.
  3. Use the table to pick your next action bundle.
